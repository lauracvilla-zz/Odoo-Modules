Opportunity on Orders and Quotations Forms

This module displays the Opportunity on Orders forms as well as on Quotations forms (In the Sales Module). Simply go to chosen Order and Opportunity will be displayed below the payment terms

How it works

Opportunity field appears below the Payment terms. If there is no connected Opportunity and empty field will be displayed.

Reports updated

Opportunity will be also listed in the printed report(pdf). In case there is no connected opportunity it won't be visible(the "Opportunity:").
