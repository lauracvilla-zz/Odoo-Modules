Opportunity on Sales Orders Forms

This module displays the Opportunity on Sales order form as well as on Quotation form. Simply go to chosen Sales Order and open the "Other Information" Tab.

How it works

Opportunity will be displayed in the "Other Information" tab of Sales Orders and Quotations forms. Please note that this module activates the hidden functionality so it is safe to use. Additionally user can click on the connected opportunity to get into opportunity details.
