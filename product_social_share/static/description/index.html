Share product on social media and by email

This simple module adds share buttons just below the "Add to cart" button.

How it works

Social share and email buttons are added to product cart. They are placed just below the "Add to cart" button. Just like on image below.
Module was tested on standard Odoo themes (Bootstrap & Bootswatch). If you installed any non-standard module please contact before you buy this app.
