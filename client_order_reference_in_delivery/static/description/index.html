Customer Reference on Delivery Form and Delivery Note

Your customer could be using other CRM/ERP and his order numeration is different. That is why there is a Customer Reference field in Sales Orders. This module extends this field to Delivery FOrm as well as to Delivery Note making your customers life easier.

Customer Reference on Delivery Form

Customer Reference is now displayed on the Delivery Form. It is editable so you can change if necessary(for example customer has changed his numeration).

Customer Reference on Delivery Slip

Customer Reference is now visible on the Delivery Slip. This makes life easier for those who prefer the traditional, paper reports.
