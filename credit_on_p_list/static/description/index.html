Partner credit visible on the list view

Thanks to this module you can see Partner's credit (Total Receivable) on the list view.
This applies to both Customers and Vendors(Suppliers) in many modules:
- Accounting
- Contacts
- Purchase
- Sales

How it works

Module is easy and safe to use. New field "Credit" will be added just behind the "email" in the List View(Tree View)
